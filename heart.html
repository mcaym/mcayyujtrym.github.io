The House of Mar was the ruling dynasty and royal family in Haven City, made up of the descendants of the city's founder Mar. The House's insignia was the Seal of Mar, indicating royalty. The members of the House ruled over Haven until the line was broken when Damas was betrayed and banished by Baron Praxis. From thereafter Baron Praxis ruled up until his death and was succeeded by his daughter Ashelin Praxis. The House of Mar was never continued, even though Damas did have a son.
